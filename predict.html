About Heart Disease Prediction

Heart disease prediction is a healthcare service that helps individuals assess their risk of heart-related ailments. This form allows users to enter personal health data, which can be analyzed using predictive algorithms to estimate the likelihood of heart disease. Please consult with a healthcare professional for a detailed analysis and diagnosis.

Prevention Tips

Here are some important tips to help prevent heart disease:

  • Maintain a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Exercise regularly to keep your heart healthy.
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques like meditation and yoga.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption.
  • Control your blood pressure and cholesterol levels.
  • Get enough sleep and maintain a healthy weight.

Health Insights

Our health insights section provides personalized suggestions based on your risk factors:

  • If your cholesterol is high, consider eating foods rich in omega-3 fatty acids and fiber.
  • If you have a sedentary lifestyle, try to incorporate at least 30 minutes of physical activity every day.
  • Monitor your blood sugar and blood pressure regularly to stay on top of your heart health.

Model Overview

Our heart disease prediction model uses a variety of machine learning algorithms to assess risk factors and predict the likelihood of heart disease. These models analyze patterns in medical data to provide accurate predictions.

Models used include:

  • Logistic Regression
  • Random Forest
  • Support Vector Machines (SVM)
  • Neural Networks
  • Gradient Boosting Machines (GBM)

Diagnostics & Testing

To diagnose heart disease, various tests are performed, such as:

  • Blood Tests (e.g., cholesterol levels, glucose)
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG)
  • Stress Testing
  • Cardiac Imaging (e.g., CT scans, MRIs)

These tests help doctors evaluate heart health and diagnose potential issues early.
